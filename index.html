Form validator

Email address requirements

  • A @ character
  • At least 2 characters before @
  • At least 2 characters after @
  • No more than one @ character
  • A dot (.) character
  • Maximum 3 dot (.) characters
  • A dot (.) is not followed by another dot (.)
  • A dot (.) and @ are not next to each other
  • Minimum 6 characters

Password requirements

  • A lowercase letter
  • A capital (uppercase) letter
  • A number
  • Minimum 8 characters

Card number requirements

  • Consists of numbers only
  • Is between 13 and 19 characters long
  • Starts with a number that is between 2 and 6
  • Passes the Luhn Formula check
